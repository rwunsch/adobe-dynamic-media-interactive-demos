Dynamic Media · Parameterized templates

One template, every variant — by URL

A designer builds the template once: layout, fonts, a stack of layers, and named parameters. After that, marketing (or an automated feed) produces unlimited variants purely through URL parameters — swap the headline, toggle which scene layer is visible. No new files, no Photoshop. This is a real template on the tenant: DMS7-Bunny-S7.

Headline

Scene layer

Each scene is a layer in the template, shown or hidden by a parameter. The bunny and the base scene stay; the overlay swaps.

Why templates

The template encapsulates the design (the bunny, the fonts, the layer stack, positioning). Consumers only supply data — $text and the *_vis toggles. Generate thousands of localized / seasonal creatives from one source of truth.
