Dynamic Media · Server-side cropping

Draw a region, get a URL

Drag a rectangle anywhere on the master below. Dynamic Media turns it into a crop=x,y,w,h modifier — in the original’s pixel space (2973 × 4460) — and renders the cut on the fly. Nothing is pre-baked: the crop happens at the edge and the unique URL is cached, so the second viewer pays nothing.
