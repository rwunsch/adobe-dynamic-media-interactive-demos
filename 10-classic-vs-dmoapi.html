Same master, two delivery APIs

One approved master asset on the RobertCo tenant, delivered side by side by two layers. Classic is Scene7 image-serving — addressed by company + asset name, the engine behind the legacy s7viewers. Dynamic Media OpenAPI is AEM-native — addressed by the asset's stable AEM ID and governed by approval + publish. Pick an asset, drag the shared sliders, and watch your browser fetch both at the chosen width and quality.
