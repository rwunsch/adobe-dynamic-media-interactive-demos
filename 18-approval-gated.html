Dynamic Media OpenAPI · Governance

Delivery is governed by approval

With Dynamic Media OpenAPI, an asset is not publicly deliverable until it's been approved in AEM (Review Status = Approved) and published. The delivery URL — by the asset's stable ID — is the same the whole time; approval flips it live. Each tile below checks its real OpenAPI delivery URL right now. Approve one in AEM, hit Re-check, and watch it turn green.

Governance lifecycle

1 · Uploaded → processed
2 · Review Status = Approved
3 · Published
4 · Live on OpenAPI delivery ✓

Until steps 2–3 are done, the public delivery endpoint returns 404 "Item doesn't exist" — content can't leak before it's signed off.

Why it matters

Governance is built into delivery itself — not bolted on. Compare with classic Scene7, where publishing is the only gate. Here, brand/legal approval in AEM is the switch that makes an asset reachable by URL, and revoking approval pulls it back.
