Why it matters

GLB / OBJ are open 3D formats — a GLB packs geometry, materials and textures into one file. The viewer is rendered with WebGL, so it runs in any modern browser with no download. Configure camera presets, lighting and turntable spin on the Viewer Preset in DM, then embed the same viewer everywhere — PDP, AR hand-off, configurator — all served and cached from the single Dynamic Media CDN.

Live Dimensional Viewer URL

The asset is the GLB on the tenant, config is the 3D Viewer Preset, and contenturl is the binary delivery endpoint (/is/content/) — the same one that streams every other rich-media file.
