Dynamic Media OpenAPI · Asset identity

Rename the file. The link never breaks.

A Dynamic Media OpenAPI delivery URL addresses an asset by its stable AEM UUID (urn:aaid:aem:…) — the asset's permanent identity. The filename in the URL (the seoname) is purely cosmetic, there for humans and SEO. Type any filename below: the image always loads, because the UUID — not the name — is what resolves the asset. Rename or move the asset in AEM and every link keeps working.

The UUID is the identity. The seoname is cosmetic — rename it to anything and the same pixels come back.

Why this matters

Classic Scene7 URLs are name-based: the asset's name is its address. Rename the asset and every link 404s.
