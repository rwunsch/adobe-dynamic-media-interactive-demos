Personalized text, warped into the scene

A reusable template renders personalized text, and the perspective= modifier maps it onto a surface in the photo — painted on the asphalt, printed on the deck chair. Type a name, then drag the four red corners to re-project the text. Every frame is rendered live by Dynamic Media from one template + one parameter set. (Public Adobe demo company.)
