Live image-serving URL (layered)

layer=N&src=is{Company/asset} stacks a foreground on the base. posN places it (center-origin, −1…+1), op_grow spreads the matte edge, and an offset dark-blurred copy underneath fakes a drop shadow. op_blur on the base blurs the background — all rendered server-side from one URL. A transparent PNG already carries its alpha (its own mask); a flat/green background would instead be knocked out with mask=…&maskUse=invert or background removal.
